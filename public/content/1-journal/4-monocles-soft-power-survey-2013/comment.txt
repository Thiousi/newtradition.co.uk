Monocle’s published it’s fourth annual (link: http://monocle.com/film/affairs/soft-power-survey-2013/ text: Soft Power Survey) - a topic that’s fascinated me ever since (link: http://mrianthomas.co.uk/post/69489420026/joseph-nye-on-the-future-of-power text: I listened to Professor Joseph Nye speak on the topic at the RSA) in London in 2011 — and Germany’s toppled the UK from its top spot.

Produced in conjunction with the (link: http://www.instituteforgovernment.org.uk/ text: Institute for Government), Monocle’s survey asks which countries ‘best attract favour from other nations through culture, sport, cuisine, design, diplomacy and beyond’. In other words: beyond the bullets, economic sanctions and so on, who packs a soft-centred punch?
